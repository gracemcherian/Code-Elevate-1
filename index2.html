Amazing Smoothies

Grace Cherian

Not only are smoothies are a great source of protein and nutrition, but they are also super easy to make. With only a few steps and even fewer necessary cookware, smoothies are the ideal meal while in a rush or when you're just too tired to cook. Smoothies are also a very creative way to make food because almost anything goes. You can have your traditional sweet fruit smoothies, like the Strawberry-Banana smoothie, or change it up with a savory twist of carrot and ginger. If you're testing your liquified limits, try our Green Machine smoothie, recipe below. With both spinach and avocado, you'll be so energized, you can't help but go on a run.

Strawberry-Banana Smoothie

This Strawberry-Banana smoothie is perfect for all ages. The creaminess brought by the yogurt and the subtle sweetness from the fruit combine to make the ideal breakfast smoothie.

Ingredients

  • 1 Frozen Banana
  • 1 cup Strawberries
  • 1/2 cup Yogurt
  • 1/2 cup Milk
  • 2 teaspoons Honey
  • Pinch Ground Cinnamon

Steps

  1. Combine milk, cinnamon, honey, strawberries, yogurt, and banana in blender
  2. Blend until mixture appears fully smooth
  3. Pour smoothie into cup and top with extra cinnamon or honey, to taste

Green Machine

The Green Machine isn't for the faint of heart. The daunting bitterness of the spinach is cut down by the sweetness of the cocunut milk and smoothness of the avocado which results in a nuritious and tasty energizing drink

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Average Avocado
  • 1/4 Cup Ice
  • 1/4 Cup Coconut Milk
  • 1/4 Cup Fresh Spinach
  • 2 Tablespoons Pistachio
  • 1 Teaspoon Vanilla Extract
  • Mint Leaves to taste

Steps

  1. Combine coconut milk, vanilla extract, avocado, spinach, mint, pistachio, and ice in a blender.
  2. Blend until no spinach chunk are visible. If too thin add more avocado or ice till desired consistency.
  3. Pour into glass and top with ground pistachio or mint leaves.
